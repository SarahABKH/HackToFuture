Book Donation Pick-Up

 Before you book a pick-up please ensure the following standards are met from your side for a quick and beneficial donation.

 Condition

 Ensure that the books you want to donate are in good condition, with no missing pages, water damage, or excessive wear and tear. Most organizations that accept book donations prefer books that are gently used and in good condition.
